This repository contains the products of RAG encodings for Claude Sonnet 4.5 and NotebookLM (Gemini 3.0) based on source materials from the [The Cybernetics Thought Collective](https://digital.library.illinois.edu/collections/38ec6eb0-18c3-0135-242c-0050569601ca-1). 



W.R. Ashby's notebooks (1928-1951) were transcribed through an NEH funded grant, sponsored by the University of Illinois, the British Library, the American Philosophical Society, and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
